About

I am a third-year undergraduate at MIT studying Artificial Intelligence and Decision Making. I am interested in machine learning, applications of AI, and questions surrounding AI risks and safety.

As an undergraduate researcher at MIT FutureTech, I have contributed to research on the use of artificial intelligence in science. My work has included building research datasets, conducting literature reviews, organizing and validating data, and using language models to extract structured information from scientific text.

Reproducibility and LLM Evaluation

Reproducing Behavioral Evaluations of Large Language Models

I am conducting a reproduction study of behavioral evaluations of large language models. This work involves reproducing model outputs, applying evaluation methods, comparing results across models and providers, and analyzing sources of variation in experimental findings.
